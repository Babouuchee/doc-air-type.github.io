What is the "Air-Type" ?

Air-Type is a game based on the series of R-Type games.

It is a 2D Shoot'em up multiplayer game that lets you control your ship to overcome enemies and vanquish the evil Bydoss !

Made in C++ with the raylib library.

It is a project made by 4 students in Bordeaux, France.
